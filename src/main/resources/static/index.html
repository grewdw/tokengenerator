TabWriter

About

TabWriter is the tool I wished I had when learning the guitar, and so I built it. It is a notebook to scribble chords, tabs, lyrics, and any other song related notes that you want.

TabWriter is designed to work with Apple Music. Even without a subscription you can create a library with any of the millions of songs that Apple Music has to offer. You can write your notes for these songs and use it free of charge.

With a subscription to Apple Music it is much more powerful. The simple interface allows you to playback songs, and also to slow the playback speed.

Privacy

TabWriter does not ask you to provide any personal information. Anything you draw in TabWriter is stored on your device and TabWriter will never transfer this elsewhere.
